What to bring to a protest:

  • Cell Phone + write a phone number on your hand (in case of arrest)
  • A buddy (stick together and make sure someone not at the protest knows where you are)
  • Mask and hand sanitizer, with extras in case (to give to others)
  • Water bottle (could also be used to rinse out eyes)
  • Comfortable clothes and shoes, including an extra set just in case
  • Cough drops (in case of voice loss during protest)
  • Rain poncho and sun protection (different weather conditions prep)
  • Goggles / eyeglasses (in case of tear gas being used by police)
  • Backpack to carry all items
  • Signs and spirit!
